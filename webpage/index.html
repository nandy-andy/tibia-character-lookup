tibia lookup character.

About

A simple Google Chrome extension which make it easy to look up a Tibia character.

Currently it is in development phase on Google Chrome and available only in developer mode (but coming soon in Chrome Web Store!)

How does it work?

After enabling the extension you notice small icon next to the address bar in Google Chrome.

It means the extension has been enabled. It also means you will find a new item in context menu of your browser after selecting any text on any page.

Let's say you are reading your guild's forum and there is a thread about a player who wants to join your guild. The creator of the thread did not think about linking the player's name in the thread. He could not do it in the thread's title, obviously!

You would open a new tab in your browser go to the character page on tibia.com, paste there the player's name and hit "Submit" button.

But you do not have to do this anymore with your tibia lookup character extension!

You just select the player's name with your mouse, click right mouse button and choose "Lookup Tibia character..."

The extension does all the work and it just opens for you a new tab with the tibia character's page.

How to install it?

The extension is in development phase. Therefore the only way to install it is to do it manually.

You download the newest version from here. Unpack it somewhere on your disk and then enable it in Google Chrome following these instructions.
